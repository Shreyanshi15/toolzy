💡 About BMI
📏
What is BMI?
Body Mass Index is weight (kg) divided by height² (m²). It gives a quick snapshot of weight status.
⚠️
BMI Limitations
BMI doesn't measure body fat directly. Muscle mass, age, gender & ethnicity all affect interpretation.
🏃
Healthy Range
A BMI of 18.5–24.9 is considered healthy for most adults. Athletes may naturally score higher.
🩺
Consult a Doctor
For a complete health picture, always consult a healthcare professional. BMI is just one metric.
