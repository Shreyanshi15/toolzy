⚠️ Eclipse Safety
🕶️Solar Eclipses: NEVER look directly at the Sun without certified eclipse glasses (ISO 12312-2). Regular sunglasses are NOT safe.
🌙Lunar Eclipses: Safe to view with naked eyes. Binoculars or a telescope enhance the experience.
📷Photography: Use a solar filter on your camera lens for solar eclipses. Remove only during totality.
🌍Visibility: Each eclipse is only visible from certain parts of Earth. Check local times and visibility maps before planning.
